Healthcare systems worldwide have been using the REVENUE CYCLE MANAGEMENT process to track the revenue from patients.   • Patient registration and demographic entry
  • Electronic claims transmission
  • Payment posting
  • Denial management
  • Account Receivable (AR) management
  • Patient calling
  • Business analytics
  • Customized reports
